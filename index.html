John van der Meule
Thesis abstract, 2015
For my thesis, I want to examine what the role of found footage is in graphic design. During my third year at the art academy, I became aware that I am also using this phenomenon in some of my projects. But where exactly does this come from? To the outside world this choice of using found footage become associated with laziness and not original but from a graphic design perspective, this could be a more logical step. But more that than, it could give an extra layer to a particular project.

Found footage is used in many ways. But since you can not identify everything as found footage, I give it some extra synonyms: Appropriating, copy-ing, stealing, use of found footage, re-use, re-taking, quoteing and duplicate.

It is not where you take things from, it is where you take them to.
Jean-Luc Godard
